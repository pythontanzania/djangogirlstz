About Django Girls

Django Girls is a community that empowers women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources, and support. We are a volunteer-run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology.

Mission

Our mission is to empower women by providing opportunities to learn and develop technical skills.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every woman has access to the knowledge and resources to thrive in the tech industry.
