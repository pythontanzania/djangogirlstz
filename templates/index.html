At Django Girls Community, we believe in the power of inspiration to ignite change. We reach out to young women through universities and coding events, offering a welcoming environment to anyone curious about technology. Who we are

Django Girls Tanzania is a community that empowers women by organizing free, one-day programming workshops. We provide participants with mentorship, resources, and a supportive environment to learn the basics of web development using Django and Python.

We are a volunteer-run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology.
